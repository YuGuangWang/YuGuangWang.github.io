About my name

My first name "Yuguang" is pronounced as "y eu-ee Kwang". In mandarin Chinese "Yuguang" is composed of two characters.

"Yu" means the universe and "guang" means "bright" or "light". My last name "Wang" is more commonly known.

My preferred abbreviation for "Yuguang Wang" is "YG. Wang".

This is the Chinese characters of my name
王 宇 光
Wang Yu Guang
